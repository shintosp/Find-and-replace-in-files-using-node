With more than 40 years of combined legal experience, our team here at The Law Offices of Thomas Maronick Jr LLC provides knowledgeable, focused legal representation if you are facing criminal charges or have suffered personal injury.

Our firm has been positioned as an authority on many subjects in a wide range of stories by prominent news outlets. When you need an accomplished criminal defense attorney in Baltimore, The Law Offices of Thomas Maronick Jr LLC offers comprehensive legal guidance and aggressive advocacy.

Why Hire Our Criminal Lawyers in Baltimore, MD?

Our award-winning attorneys educate each of our clients on what is best for their specific case. We are proud to share that The Law Offices of Thomas Maronick recently received the Best Lawyer of 2019 from Birdeye.
