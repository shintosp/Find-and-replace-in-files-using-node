Baltimore Family Law Attorney

Your Divorce Advocates in Maryland

Our firm understands how important family matters are, especially when children are involved. Our Baltimore family lawyers provide personalized attention to clients who choose our firm to guide them through this tumultuous time. Whether you need legal help in dividing marital assets or creating a custody schedule for your children, an experienced and passionate advocate from our firm is on your side.

Our family attorneys take the time to get to know you, understand your needs, and implement the legal measures necessary to help you achieve your goals. We are also committed to providing easily understandable advice so you can make informed decisions regarding the future of your family.

Call us today at (410) 934-3007 to discuss your matter.

Legal Services We Offer

We represent clients facing a wide range of family law issues such as:

  • Divorce - including property division, child support, and alimony
  • Child custody and visitation matters - including modifications
  • Paternity - assistance with custody, visitation, and establishing paternity and child support matters for unwed mothers and fathers
  • Protective orders - protecting the safety of victims of domestic violence or those falsely accused of domestic violence through securing or opposing the issuance of a protective order
  • Annulments - helping navigate the specific legal requirements in annulments cases
  • Separation agreements - drafting a contract between a husband and wife who choose to live separately that makes decisions regarding property division, child custody, child support, spousal support, and other key issues

Turn to Our Divorce & Family Law Attorneys in Baltimore

Our main goal is to do what is necessary to promote the well-being of you and your family while minimizing any additional emotional trauma during a stressful legal experience. We will work to resolve disputed matters efficiently and peacefully, but are also willing and prepared to fight for you in the courtroom if necessary.
